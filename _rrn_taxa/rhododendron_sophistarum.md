## Notes
This placement within the Rhododendron infrageneric classification is currently known.

A new name wass required as the epithet _pauciflorum_ is pre-empted in Rhododendron by _R. pauciflorum_ King & Gamble. The new epithet is derived from the Greek, _sophistes_, master, expert, and honours those who strive to understand the natural world.
