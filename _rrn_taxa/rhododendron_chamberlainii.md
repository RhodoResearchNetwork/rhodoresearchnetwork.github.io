## Notes
This name used to be in the genus Diplarche. Its formal place in the _Rhdododendron_ infrageneric classification is so far unknonwn.

A new name was required as the epithet _multiflorum_ is pre-empted in Rhododendron by _R. multiflorum_ (Maxim.) Craven. The replacement epithet honours David Franklin Chamberlain (1941–), Royal Botanic Garden, Edinburgh, who has contributed significantly to our understanding of the taxonomy of Rhododendron.
