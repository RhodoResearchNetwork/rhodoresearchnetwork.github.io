## Description
Small, evergreen shrub to 2 m. Young shoots densely lepidote. Leaves narrowly elliptic, obovate or rarely lanceolate, coriaceous or subcoriaceous, (6-)10-24(-30) x (3-)4-12(-16) mm, dark green and usually densely lepidote with variably persistent scales above, pale greyish green beneath with distant to overlapping, large, brownish scales with translucent rims. Inflorescences 1-2-flowered, pedicels lepidote, (10-)12-25 mm. Calyx lobes variable in shape, ovate or oblong, rarely spathulate, rounded, (2-)3-4 mm, greenish or reddish, lepidote. Corolla white, yellow, pink, red or various shades of purple, often spotted darker, (10-)12-15(-17) mm, tube to 7(-8) mm, usually densely lepidote outside. Ovary lepidote. Style very short, deflexed.
