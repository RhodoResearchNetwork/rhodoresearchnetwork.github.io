## Description
Shrub to 2 m. Young shoots loriform-setose, the setae persistent for at least one year. Leaves narrowly obovate, 70-80 x 25-30 mm, tapering to the base, bluntly acute at the apex, lower surface brown with dense but not contiguous scales. Inflorescence (2-)3-4-flowered. Calyx obscurely lobed or undulate, persistently loriform-setose. Corolla white, funnel-campanulate, c. 55 mm, tube c. 30 mm, pilose at the base, the whole surface sparingly lepidote.
