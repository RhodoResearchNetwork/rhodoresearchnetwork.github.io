## Description
Shrub or small tree, 3-10m. Leaves oblong-obovate to oblanceolate, (14-)20-30(-40) x 5.2-12cm, 2.2-2.8(-3.6) x as long as broad, apex rounded, base cuneate, upper surface glabrous, lower surface with a silvery compacted and agglutinated indumentum; petioles l-2cm, strongly flattened and winged, sparsely tomentose to glabrescent. Inflorescence 12-20-flowered; rhachis up to 25mm; pedicels 30-40mm, sparsely floccose-tomentose. Calyx l-2mm, tomentose. Corolla 7-8-lobed, obliquely campanulate, pale yellow or white flushed with pink, to pink with crimson flecks and a basal blotch, 35â€”50mm. Stamens c. 16. Ovary covered with a dense buff tomentum. Capsule 30-40 x 6-10mm, usually curved.
