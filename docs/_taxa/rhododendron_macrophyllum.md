## Description
Shrub, 2-4m; young shoots soon Â± glabrous. Leaves broadly elliptic, (6.5-)8.5-12(-17) x 3-5.2(-7.5)cm, 2.5-2.8 x as long as broad, apex acute to minutely apiculate, base cuneate, upper and lower surfaces glabrous when mature; petioles l-2(-3)cm, glabrous. Inflorescence 10-20-flowered; rhachis 15-30mm; pedicels glabrous, 30-60mm in flower, hardly elongating in fruit. Calyx c.lmm, glabrous. Corolla broadly campanulate, white to pink with yellowish flecks, 30-40mm. Ovary densely rufous-pilose; style glabrous. Capsule c.25 x 7mm.

## Distribution
Western Seaboard of USA from the Canadian border to California. Forest margins, etc.
